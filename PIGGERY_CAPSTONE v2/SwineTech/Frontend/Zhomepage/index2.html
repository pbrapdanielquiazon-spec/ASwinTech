FAQ

Do I need internet to use SwineTech?

For now, yes. An offline-first mode is on our roadmap for field use with spotty connectivity.

Who can use the system?

Admin, Staff, and Caretakers (internal), plus a Guest view for buyers to browse available pigs.

Is my farm data secure?

Accounts are protected with tokens, and sensitive operations require authentication. OTP-based email verification is enabled.

What features are available for customers?

Viewing available pigs, manage bookings, manage receipts, and guest listings.

Can I book or reserve pigs as a guest?

Guests can only browse available pigs, booking and checkout require registration and login.
